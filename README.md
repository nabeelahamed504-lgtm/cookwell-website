# Cookwell Food Company

Official website for Cookwell Food Company by SRP Traders.

## About

Cookwell Food Company offers premium 100% pure wheat-based food products, including macaroni, pasta, noodles, and vermicelli. Our mission is to provide healthy, quality food products with reliable delivery across Tamil Nadu.

This website serves as an online product showcase, allowing customers to explore products, view pricing, and place orders directly through WhatsApp.

## Features

- Product catalog with images and pricing
- Quantity selection for orders
- WhatsApp ordering integration
- Delivery across Tamil Nadu
- Salem location map
- Contact and social media information
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop

## Tech Stack

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
